Essential Wakeboarding Gear

1. Wakeboards - $300-$600

Larger boards (140-150cm) are more forgiving. Look for continuous rocker and medium flex.

2. Life Vest - $150-$300

Coast Guard approved. Non-negotiable for safety. Choose wakeboard-specific vests.

3. Helmet - $100-$250

Water sports helmet with buoyant design. Essential when learning new tricks.

4. Rope & Handle - $80-$200

Standard: 55-60 feet. Beginners start at 30-50 feet. Single handle easier for beginners.
